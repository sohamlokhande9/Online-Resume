Who I Am

Innovator by Night

I'm Soham Lokhande, a First-Year Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering student at MIT Academy of Engineering, Alandi, Pune. My passion lives at the intersection of hardware and software — from blinking LEDs to building autonomous robots.

As Co-founder of Binary Botz, I'm turning ideas into impact, building a startup focused on robotics and intelligent embedded systems. I believe the engineers who bridge the physical and digital world will define the next decade.

When I'm not soldering circuits or writing code, I'm learning something new — because curiosity is the most valuable tool in any engineer's kit.
